Research Projects

2025 – Present
Understanding Space Weather Effects on Exospheric Escape from Earth

  • Study of Geocorona using Lyman Alpha Emission
  • Changes on Earth's exosphere during geomagnetic storm events (high negative Dst index)

2024 – 2025
High Energy Astrophysics — Gamma Ray Bursts

Supervisor: Dr. Resmi L., Associate Professor, IIST (Remote)

  • Studied how GRB hardness and duration vary with observer's viewing angle
  • Modelled(Computational) single-pulse GRBs using a geometrically and optically thin top-hat jet model
  • Simulated bursts with isotropic viewing angles in FERMI-GBM bands
  • Established strong dependence of spectral hardness on viewing angle
