About This Course

Designed for absolute beginners who want to learn the core concepts of programming. The emphasis is on learning how to think like a programmer, not just memorising syntax. By the end, you will have the confidence to write basic programs, debug errors, read documentation, and tackle real problems — with or without AI assistance.

Includes 100+ practice questions and online assistance. Recordings of all live sessions are available.
