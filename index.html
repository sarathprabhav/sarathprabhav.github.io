About

Bridging Research & Education

Freelance educator and science enthusiast with a passion for promoting scientific thinking. Research

Current & Past Projects

2025 – Present
Space Weather & Exospheric Escape

Studying solar activity effects on Earth's upper atmosphere via Lyman Alpha emission. Using the NRLMSIS model to simulate atmospheric parameters and validate against GOLD satellite measurements during geomagnetic storm events.

Dr. Dolon Bhattacharya · LASP

2024 – 2025
Gamma-Ray Burst Viewing Angle Effects

Modelled single-pulse GRBs using a geometrically thin top-hat jet model. Established strong dependence of spectral hardness on viewing angle for broken power-law spectra via FERMI-GBM band simulations.

Dr. Resmi L. · IIST
